For over 15 years, Shri Sai Bridal Jewels has adorned the brides of Thoothukudi with jewellery that honours Tamil tradition. Every piece we make or curate is a bridge between generations — carrying the weight of ritual and the light of new beginnings.

Located in the heart of Ezhil Nagar, our atelier offers both purchase and premium rental jewellery — making heirloom-quality gold accessible to every family.
